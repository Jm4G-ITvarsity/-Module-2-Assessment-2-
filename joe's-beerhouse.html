Joe's Beerhouse

About us: Step into the lively atmosphere of Joe's Beerhouse, an iconic Namibian destination known for its hearty meals, rustic charm, and a wide variety of local and international beers. With its warm, welcoming vibe and rich history, Joe's is the perfect place to gather with friends and family to enjoy traditional Namibian dishes like eisbein, kudu steaks, and schnitzels. Whether you're looking for a casual bite or a unique dining experience, Joe's Beerhouse offers something for everyone.

Working Hours:

  • Monday to Saturday: 11:00 AM – 11:00 PM
  • Sunday: 11:00 AM – 9:00 PM

Joe's Beerhouse is celebrated for its relaxed, eclectic decor and a menu that combines Namibian classics with hearty German-inspired cuisine. The beer selection is vast, featuring craft and draft beers from Namibia, Germany, and beyond. Famous for its lively social scene and great service, Joe's is a must-visit for both locals and tourists alike. Whether you're unwinding after a long day or celebrating with friends, Joe's Beerhouse is the place to enjoy great food, drinks, and a memorable atmosphere.
