Levi's Store

About us: Find your perfect fit at Levi's Store, the home of iconic denim and timeless style. Known for its classic jeans, jackets, and casual apparel, Levi's offers a range of fits and washes that cater to every taste, from the classic 501® to modern skinny and slim styles. In addition to denim, explore a selection of shirts, t-shirts, and accessories designed for those who value comfort and authentic style. Levi's combines heritage with contemporary trends, ensuring you always stay effortlessly stylish..

Working Hours:

  • Sunday & Public Holidays: 9:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Levi's is synonymous with quality and durability, offering clothing that stands the test of time. The store also provides personalized services such as denim alterations and customization options, allowing you to make your Levi's uniquely yours. Whether you're shopping for a new pair of jeans or a classic denim jacket, knowledgeable staff are available to help you find the perfect fit and style.
