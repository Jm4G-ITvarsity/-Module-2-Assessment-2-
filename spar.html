Spar

About us: Shop fresh and local at Spar, your neighborhood supermarket known for quality products and friendly service. Spar offers a wide range of groceries, from farm-fresh produce and premium meats to household essentials and everyday staples. Whether you're stocking up on pantry items, grabbing a quick meal, or shopping for specialty products, Spar provides convenience and variety to meet all your grocery needs.

Working Hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM – 7:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Sunday & Public Holidays: 8:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Spar is dedicated to supporting local suppliers while offering a wide selection of international brands. The store’s bakery, deli, and butchery sections provide freshly prepared foods, while the fresh produce section features locally sourced fruits and vegetables. With regular specials and a customer loyalty program, Spar ensures a rewarding shopping experience for all. Friendly staff are always available to help you find what you need and make your shopping trip more enjoyable.
