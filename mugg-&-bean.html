Mugg & Bean

About us: Fuel your day with wholesome meals and bottomless coffee at Mugg & Bean, a popular café known for its generous portions and friendly service. Offering a variety of delicious breakfast options, hearty lunches, and indulgent desserts, Mugg & Bean is the perfect spot to meet friends, get some work done, or simply enjoy a comforting meal. From freshly baked muffins and gourmet sandwiches to health-conscious salads and wraps, there's something for everyone.

Working Hours:

  • Monday to Friday: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • Sunday: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Mugg & Bean is loved for its warm and welcoming atmosphere, where guests can enjoy bottomless coffee refills and an extensive menu of mouth-watering options. Whether you’re stopping by for a quick coffee break or a leisurely meal, Mugg & Bean provides a comfortable space with plenty of choices to satisfy your cravings. Popular menu items include their famous giant muffins, classic burgers, and a variety of vegan and vegetarian options. With consistent quality and excellent service, Mugg & Bean is a go-to for casual dining and great coffee experiences.
