Barbie

Description: In this vibrant and fantastical comedy, Barbie (Margot Robbie) lives in the perfect world of Barbie Land, where everything is flawless and everyone is happy. However, when Barbie begins to question her own identity and purpose, she embarks on an adventure into the real world, alongside her charming companion, Ken (Ryan Gosling). The journey uncovers unexpected truths and hilarious encounters as Barbie explores what it truly means to be human and embrace imperfection. Directed by Greta Gerwig, this visually stunning film blends humor, heart, and a nostalgic nod to the beloved Barbie brand.

Main Cast: Margot Robbie as Barbie, Ryan Gosling as Ken, America Ferrera, Simu Liu, Will Ferrell
