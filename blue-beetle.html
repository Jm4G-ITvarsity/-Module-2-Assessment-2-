Blue Beetle

Description: When Jaime Reyes, a recent college graduate, unexpectedly comes into possession of an ancient alien relic known as the Scarab, his life changes forever. The Scarab chooses Jaime to become its symbiotic host, granting him powerful armor and superhuman abilities, transforming him into the Blue Beetle. As Jaime struggles to understand his new identity and powers, he must protect his family and face powerful enemies who seek to control the Scarab's immense power. This superhero origin story blends action, heart, and a deep exploration of heritage, identity, and responsibility.
