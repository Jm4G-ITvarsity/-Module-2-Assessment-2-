The Olive Restaurant

About us: Experience fine dining in a serene and stylish setting at The Olive Restaurant, where gourmet cuisine meets modern elegance. Nestled within the tranquil ambiance of The Olive Exclusive Hotel, this restaurant offers a carefully crafted menu that blends local ingredients with international flavors. From beautifully plated dishes to a curated selection of wines, The Olive Restaurant provides an unforgettable dining experience that tantalizes the senses.

Working Hours:

  • Monday to Saturday: 7:00 AM – 10:00 PM
  • Sunday: 7:00 AM – 2:00 PM

The Olive Restaurant is renowned for its fusion of contemporary and traditional culinary styles, featuring dishes like tender lamb, fresh seafood, and vegetarian options. With a focus on seasonal, locally sourced ingredients, every meal is prepared with precision and creativity. The restaurant's intimate atmosphere, coupled with exceptional service, makes it an ideal spot for romantic dinners, business lunches, or special celebrations. Enjoy the refined yet welcoming experience as you explore a diverse menu designed to delight your palate.
