Gran Turismo

Description: Gran Turismo is based on the inspiring true story of a young gamer whose incredible skills at the popular racing simulation game lead him into the world of professional motorsports. With the support of a former racecar driver turned mentor, the young man must prove that his virtual racing abilities can translate to the real world, all while navigating intense competition, high-speed tracks, and personal challenges. This film showcases the journey from gaming to racing, blending heart-pounding action with a story of determination and passion.

Main Cast: Archie Madekwe as Aspiring Racer ,David Harbour as Former Racecar Driver ,Orlando Bloom as Racing Team Owner ,Darren Barnet as Rival Driver
